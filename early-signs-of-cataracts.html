Frequently Asked Questions

What is better laser or LASIK?

LASIK is a type of laser eye surgery. Other laser eye surgeries include PRK (Photorefractive Keratectomy) and SMILE (Small Incision Lenticule Extraction). The choice between them depends on factors like corneal thickness and the patient's specific visual needs.

Can you blink during laser eye?

Patients may have involuntary eye movements, including blinking, during laser eye surgery. However, advanced tracking systems in the lasers used for procedures like LASIK help ensure precise treatment despite these movements.

Is there something better than LASIK?

Different procedures may be more suitable for certain individuals or specific eye conditions. For example, PRK or SMILE may be preferred over LASIK in some cases. Consultation with an eye care professional is essential to determine the most appropriate option.
