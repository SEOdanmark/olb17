What is the safest eye surgery?

Many eye surgeries are considered safe when performed by experienced surgeons. However, the safety of a procedure depends on various factors such as the patient's overall health, the specific condition being treated, and the skill of the surgeon.

Which countries do the best eye surgery?

Several countries are known for their advanced medical facilities and skilled ophthalmologists, including the United States, Germany, Japan, South Korea, and Singapore.

Does LASIK eye surgery hurt?

LASIK is typically painless as numbing drops are used to keep the eye comfortable during the procedure. Some patients may experience mild discomfort or pressure sensation, but significant pain is rare.
