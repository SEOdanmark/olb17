Frequently Asked Questions

Is LASIK eye surgery worth it?

For many people, LASIK can significantly improve vision and quality of life. However, whether it's "worth it" depends on individual factors such as eye health, lifestyle, and expectations, which should be discussed with an eye care professional.

Should I get LASIK at 35?

LASIK can be a suitable option for individuals in their thirties, including those aged 35. However, candidacy depends on various factors, including eye health, stability of vision prescription, and overall health, which should be evaluated by an eye care professional.

What happens if I blink during LASIK?

During LASIK, an instrument called a speculum holds the eyelids open, preventing blinking. Additionally, the laser has a tracking system that pauses the procedure if eye movement is detected.
