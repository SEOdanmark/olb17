Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most popular eye surgery?

LASIK eye surgery is among the most popular vision correction procedures globally due to its effectiveness in treating refractive errors like nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism.

What are the 3 types of eye surgery?

There are various types of eye surgeries, but three common ones include LASIK (Laser-Assisted In Situ Keratomileusis) for vision correction, cataract surgery to remove cloudy lenses, and glaucoma surgery to relieve pressure inside the eye.

Who Cannot have laser eye surgery?

Individuals with certain eye conditions, unstable vision, or systemic health issues may not be suitable candidates for laser eye surgery. An eye care professional can evaluate each individual case to determine candidacy.

Can you cry with LASIK?

Yes, you can cry after LASIK. However, excessive rubbing of the eyes should be avoided, especially during the initial healing period, to prevent dislodging the corneal flap.
