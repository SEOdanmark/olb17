Frequently Asked Questions

Is eye surgery high risk?

While all surgeries carry some level of risk, many eye surgeries, including LASIK, are considered low-risk procedures when performed by qualified professionals. Serious complications are rare but possible and should be discussed with your surgeon.

What is the best age to get LASIK eye surgery?

The best age for LASIK varies for each person but is typically when the prescription has stabilized, usually in the late teens to early twenties. However, older adults can still be candidates for LASIK if they meet other criteria.

Will LASIK change my life?

LASIK can significantly improve vision and quality of life for many people by reducing or eliminating the need for glasses or contact lenses. However, individual experiences may vary, and it's essential to have realistic expectations about the outcome.
